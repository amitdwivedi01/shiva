Group of Companies

Shiva Pharmachem Limited

Shiva Pharmachem Limited, which is involved in chlorination chemistry and is manufacturing intermediates for the pharmaceutical, agrochemical and polymer industry.

Our extensive expertise in chlorination chemistry allows us to produce a wide range of specialized intermediates that play a vital role in various industries. These intermediates serve as essential building blocks for the synthesis of pharmaceutical compounds, agrochemical formulations, and polymer additives.

Shiva Engineering Services

Shiva Engineering Services (SES) is one of the leading providers of process plant engineering and construction services. This wide spectrum of services includes planning, design & engineering, procurement, construction & execution as well as industrial services and consulting. Our experience comes from serving a broad range of reputed and industry leading manufacturers and multi-national around the globe across multiple markets.

SES engages with customers right from concept till start-up of a project with comprehensive involvement and support under one roof, whether the project is large or small, greenfield or brown-field, re-vamp or modification. SES brings on the table its full-service capabilities on each project engagement
